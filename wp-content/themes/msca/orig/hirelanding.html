Hire Our Coders

Our training program focuses on the following:

Full stack development

Our coders are full stack and become proficient in front end, back end, databases and MV* patterns.

Simulated workplace

Industry like hours: ~9 AM to 5 PM, five days a week, for 11-months.

Employer Evaluation & Review

Employers input for curriculum design and structure. They also provide resources to assist in instruction, mentoring, and evaluations.

Our coders learn and are exposed to the following technologies and languages:

  • Encapsulation & Inheritance
  • Databases & Normalization
  • SQL & Connection Strings
  • JavaScript
  • React & MVC
  • HTML/ CSS
  • Git
  • Algorithms
  • C# & .Net
  • Node.js
  • PHP
  • Python
  • Java
  • Asp.net

Our coders learn soft skills by living the workplace and lectures. They have team projects and interact with the instructors as managers and coders as co-workers.
